# Space Member Playbook

## Join by Linkup
- **Invite Requirement:** Secure an invite code from a current member to join D1, an invite-only platform.
- **Registration:** Link your X account and digital wallet during sign-up.
- **Fee and Rewards:** Pay a 0.005 ETH joining fee to receive D1 gems and Blast Gold. Gems convert to OLE bi-weekly, and Blast Gold to $Blast in May.
- **Bonus Gems:** Earn 50% extra gems by sharing your joining excitement on X.

## Invite with Instant Reward
- **Rewards for Inviting:** Receive instant rewards for every successful referral who signs up and pays the joining fee.
- **Invite Process:** Use your unique invite link from the D1 dashboard for invitations.

## Being Active and Collect Rewards in Spaces
- **Daily Rallies:** Join rallies three times a day for a chance to win the jackpot.
- **Bootstrap Sessions:** Earn more gems by participating in space bootstrap sessions; rewards increase with more active members.
- **Social Engagement:** Earn gems by giving and receiving likes within the community.

## Buying Share
- **Invest in Spaces:** Purchase shares in spaces to gain financial stakes and potentially earn dividends or have voting rights.

## Speculate and Support Your Space in Sprint Section
- **Engage in Sprints:** Support your favorite spaces and speculate on their success to earn rewards.

## Boost Your Earnings with sOLE
- **Staking OLE:** Convert OLE to sOLE by staking, enhancing your earnings through additional rewards and benefits.

## How Do Your Gems Convert to OLE
- **Conversion Mechanism:** Automatically convert earned gems to OLE bi-weekly, with rates communicated in advance.

## Compound Your Reward
- **Reinvest Rewards:** Members are encouraged to reinvest their earnings to maximize their potential returns through compounding strategies.
